Our story

Built where the need was greatest.
Built for everywhere it exists.

EmpowerBot started in a Lagos market. It runs on the evidence that small businesses everywhere deserve access to the same quality of learning infrastructure that large enterprises take for granted.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on

AI is a democratisation tool

The same AI that powers billion-dollar companies can explain business concepts in Pidgin to a market trader in Kano — and that is not a side feature. That is the mission.

Outcomes, not attendance

A programme that can't prove what changed in its participants can't sustain funding. We exist to turn training sessions into verifiable evidence of knowledge change.

Zero friction for learners

If your training requires participants to download an app, create an account, or own a smartphone, you've already lost half your cohort. WhatsApp is infrastructure.

Where it started

A problem in Lagos. A platform for the world.

The problem
MSME training wasn't sticking
Running training across Lagos LGAs in partnership with LSETF, completion rates were low and participants forgot lessons within days. Printed handouts. Classroom sessions. None of it translated to behaviour change that a donor could point to.
The experiment · 2025
What if training lived in WhatsApp?
ELP Foundation moved the training to WhatsApp. Quizzes via button messages. AI explanations on wrong answers. A live leaderboard. Completion rate jumped to 89%. 1,000+ participants. Top score: 99%.
The insight · 2025–2026
The data was worth more than the training
Funders didn't just appreciate the completion rates — they needed the structured outcome data. EmpowerBot wasn't just a training tool. It was an evidence platform. That changed everything about how we thought about what we were building.

Global by design

Built for any programme. Anywhere small businesses operate.

EmpowerBot's AI adapts to language, currency, and business context. It isn't a Lagos product wearing a global hat — the evidence model, the WhatsApp delivery, and the AI explanations work wherever small businesses need support.
