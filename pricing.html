FAQ

Common questions

How does the free nonprofit plan work?

Submit your organisation's registration document and we'll verify within 48 hours. Once verified, you get full platform access permanently — no hidden fees, no feature restrictions.

What counts as an "active participant"?

A participant who has completed at least one quiz in the current billing month. Inactive participants don't count. You can archive old cohorts to free up slots.

Is the AI included on the free plan?

Yes. AI-powered explanations, follow-up question support, and AI review sessions are all included on every plan including the free nonprofit tier. The AI is not a paid add-on.

Can I add custom quiz content?

Custom quiz content is available on Growth and Enterprise plans. Nonprofit plan users get access to the full library of 21 modules.
