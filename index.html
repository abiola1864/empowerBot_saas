AI-first by design

The AI that explains it
in their language.

EmpowerBot doesn't just quiz — it teaches. Every wrong answer triggers a personalised AI explanation using the learner's business type, city, and local currency. Not a Wikipedia definition.

  • Context-aware explanationsThe AI knows their business sector, location, and history. Every explanation is different.
  • Natural follow-up questionsParticipants type "but why?" and the AI answers in context — no dead ends.
  • Pidgin, local currency, real scenariosNot generic examples. Their city, their market, their naira amounts.
  • Wrong-answer review sessionsAfter every quiz, the AI walks through every missed question with a fresh explanation.

When you be sole proprietor, you and your business na the SAME person under law. So if your provisions shop borrow ₦50,000 and you no fit pay, dem fit collect your personal savings, your phone, even your house!

But LLC na like putting a fence between you and your business 🏡

For a provisions shop in Lagos with 1–2 staff, LLC registration at CAC costs about ₦25,000–₦50,000 and protects your personal assets. Given you already have stock worth N100k+, it's worth it.
