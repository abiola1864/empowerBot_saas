The AI that explains it in their language.

EmpowerBot doesn't just quiz — it teaches. Every wrong answer triggers a personalised AI explanation using the learner's business type, city, and local currency.

  • Context-aware explanationsThe AI knows their business, location, and history. Every explanation is different.
  • Natural follow-up questionsParticipants type "but why?" and the AI answers in full context — no dead ends.
