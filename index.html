Frequently asked questions

Do participants need their own WhatsApp Business account?

No. Participants just message EmpowerBot's WhatsApp number using their regular WhatsApp. No business registration needed.

How does nonprofit verification work?

Submit your CAC certificate, NGO registration, or equivalent documentation. We review within 2 business days and activate your free account.

Can I upload my own quiz content?

Yes. Upload quizzes via the admin panel interface or our simple JSON format. We also offer content development support for enterprise clients.

What languages does the AI support?

English, Nigerian Pidgin, and Yoruba are currently available. Hausa and Igbo are in development. Custom language support available on Enterprise.

Our story

Built in Lagos, for Lagos entrepreneurs

EmpowerBot was built to solve a real problem. Running MSME training across Lagos LGAs in partnership with LSETF, completion rates were low and participants forgot lessons within days.

Moving training to WhatsApp changed everything. Completion rates reached 89 per cent. AI explanations in Pidgin English made concepts stick in ways printed handouts never could.

Our founding partner

EmpowerBot is built and maintained by ELP Foundation and delivered in partnership with the Lagos State Employment Trust Fund. LSETF provides institutional backing and beneficiary access. ELP Foundation manages the platform, quiz curriculum, and data systems.

Partnership timeline

From pilot to platform

2025 Pilot
LSETF/ELP Foundation MSME Bootcamp
EmpowerBot deployed across Epe, Ikorodu, and Alimosho. Approximately 120 entrepreneurs completed quiz-based training on business structuring, financial records, and digital marketing. Completion rate: 89 per cent. Top score: 99 per cent.
2026 Current
MSME Capacity Building, Agege LGA
EmpowerBot is the primary learning tool for the current LSETF/ELP programme running every Thursday from 26 March to 16 April 2026. Topics cover business values and mindset, LSETF loan products, digital marketing, and financial management.
2026 Onwards
Opening to other organisations
Following the LSETF success, ELP Foundation is making EmpowerBot available to other NGOs, government programmes, and social enterprises. Nonprofits access the full platform for free.
What EmpowerBot delivers

The digital backbone of the programme

Participant onboarding
Every participant self-registers via WhatsApp. Business type, location, age, and main challenge are captured automatically and feed M&E reporting.
Quiz-based knowledge assessment
Participants complete quiz modules per training topic. Results per question give facilitators a clear picture of knowledge gaps across the full cohort.
AI-powered reinforcement
Wrong answers trigger personalised AI explanations in simple English and Pidgin, using Naira amounts and examples from the participant's own business type.
Live data for reporting
The admin dashboard gives real-time access to completion rates, scores, and wrong answer patterns. All exportable for M&E submissions.
