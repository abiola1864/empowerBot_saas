Frequently asked questions

Do participants need their own WhatsApp Business account?

No. Participants just message EmpowerBot's WhatsApp number using their regular WhatsApp. No business registration needed.

How does nonprofit verification work?

Submit your CAC certificate, NGO registration, or equivalent documentation. We review within 2 business days and activate your free account.

Can I upload my own quiz content?

Yes. Upload quizzes via the admin panel interface or our simple JSON format. We also offer content development support for enterprise clients.

What languages does the AI support?

English, Nigerian Pidgin, and Yoruba are currently available. Hausa and Igbo are in development. Custom language support available on Enterprise.

Our story

Built in the field, for the field

EmpowerBot was born out of frustration. Running MSME training programmes across Lagos LGAs, we kept hitting the same wall: participants would show up once, then disappear. The apps were too heavy. The websites required logins. The PDFs went unread.

Then we tried WhatsApp. Completion rates went from 40% to 89% overnight. The AI explanations in Pidgin English made concepts click. We knew we had something worth building properly.
