Common questions

Do participants need WhatsApp Business?

No. Participants use their regular WhatsApp to message EmpowerBot's number. No business account or registration needed.

How does nonprofit verification work?

Submit your CAC certificate, NGO registration, or government documentation. We review within 2 business days and activate your free account.

Can I upload my own quiz content?

Yes. Upload via the admin panel or our simple JSON format. Content development support is available for Enterprise clients.

What languages does the AI support?

English and Nigerian Pidgin are live. Yoruba is in beta. Hausa and Igbo are in development. Custom language support on Enterprise.

Our story

Built in Lagos, for Lagos entrepreneurs

EmpowerBot came out of a real problem. Running MSME training across Lagos LGAs in partnership with LSETF, completion rates were low and participants forgot lessons within days.

Moving training to WhatsApp changed everything. Completion reached 89%. AI explanations in Pidgin made concepts stick in ways printed handouts never could.

Our founding partner

EmpowerBot is built and maintained by ELP Foundation and delivered in partnership with the Lagos State Employment Trust Fund. LSETF provides institutional backing and beneficiary access. ELP Foundation manages the platform, quiz curriculum, and data systems.
