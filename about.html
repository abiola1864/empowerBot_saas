What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on

AI is a democratisation tool

The same AI that powers billion-dollar companies can explain business concepts in Pidgin to a market trader in Kano — that is not a side feature. That is the mission.

Outcomes, not attendance

A programme that can't prove what changed in its participants can't sustain funding. We turn training sessions into verifiable evidence of knowledge change.

Zero friction for learners

If training requires participants to download an app or create an account, you've already lost half your cohort. WhatsApp is infrastructure. We build on it.

Where it started

A problem in Lagos.
A platform for the world.

The problem
MSME training wasn't sticking
Running training across Lagos LGAs with LSETF, completion rates were low. Printed handouts. Classroom sessions. None of it translated to behaviour change a donor could point to.
The experiment · 2025
What if training lived in WhatsApp?
ELP Foundation moved training to WhatsApp. Quizzes via button messages. AI explanations on wrong answers. A live leaderboard. Completion rate jumped to 89%. 1,000+ participants.
The insight · 2025–2026
The data was worth more than the training
Funders didn't just appreciate the completion rates — they needed the structured outcome data. EmpowerBot wasn't just a training tool. It was an evidence platform. That changed everything.
